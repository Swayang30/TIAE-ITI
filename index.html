Fee Structure & Financial Aid

At Tagore Institute of Art and Education Private ITI, we believe quality vocational training should be financially accessible to every deserving student from rural West Bengal.

Government Fee Concession

Students belonging to SC/ST categories may be eligible for government fee waivers under applicable West Bengal state schemes. Our counsellors will assist you in completing the application.

Merit-Based Scholarship

Top-performing students from Madhyamik examinations may receive partial fee concession from the institute. Academic excellence is recognised and rewarded at TIAE ITI.

WB Student Credit Scheme

Students may be eligible for government credit schemes under the West Bengal Student Credit Card Scheme. Our counsellors will guide you through the application process step by step.

Instalment Payment Option

Admission fee can be paid in instalments for students from financially weaker backgrounds, subject to management approval. We ensure no deserving student is denied education due to financial constraints.

* All scholarship and concession decisions are subject to available funds and management approval. Contact the admissions office for the latest fee schedule.
